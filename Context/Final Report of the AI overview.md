This is the overview for the AI report  
   
The report made using latex and will include and be graded by:

1. Abstract \- Giving an overview of your project.  
2. Introduction \- Introduce the problem, give an overview of the solution techniques you are using, and the evaluation you performed.  
3. Related Work \- Briefly discuss related papers in the area. If you are trying to implement methods from a paper, include it here.  
4. Methodology \- Provide a discussion of the primary method or system you were building as part of the project.  
5. Evaluation \- Provide details of the experiments you ran. Provide a clear discussion of the hypotheses you wanted to test and any baselines you may have used. Present your results using visualizations and tables when appropriate.  
6. Conclusion and Future Work \- Provide a brief summary of what you learned from the project and what you would want to do as next steps.
